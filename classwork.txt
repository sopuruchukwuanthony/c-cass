Write a C# console application that simulates a store checkout system.

Requirements
1. Prompt the user to enter the following:
Item name (string)
Price per item (float)
Quantity (int)

2. Validate the input:
If the item name is empty or contains only spaces, display
Invalid item name entered and terminate the program.

If the price is less than or equal to 0, display
Price must be greater than zero and terminate the program.

If the quantity is less than or equal to 0, display
Quantity must be greater than zero and terminate the program.

3. Calculate the total cost before discount using:
total = price * quantity

4.Apply discount rules:                                             

If total ≥ 50,000 → 15% discount
If total ≥ 20,000 → 10% discount
If total ≥ 10,000 → 5% discount
Otherwise → 0% discount
If quantity ≥ 10, add an additional 2% discount
The total discount must not exceed 20% 

5. Calculate:
Discount amount
Final total after discount

6. Display the output in the format below (rounded to 2 decimal places):
========= STORE RECEIPT =========
Item Name: Bag
Unit Price: ₦15000.00
Quantity: 2
---------------------------------
Subtotal: ₦30000.00
Discount Applied: 10%
Discount Amount: ₦3000.00
---------------------------------
Final Total: ₦27000.00
=================================
